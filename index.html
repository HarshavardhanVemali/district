WELCOME DISTRICT V201 A

A need was strongly felt to bring all Vysya Community to a common platform and to enable them to exchange their ideas and views and also to felicitate Arya Vysyas in those days who were qualified as IAS & as Doctor, Engineer & Advocates as well as those who visited Foreign Countries. With these modest aims and objectives the FIRST VASAVI CLUB was started in the year 1961 on 1st October with Eleven members at Hyderabad by Late Sri Kalvakuntla Chandrasena Gupta (K.C.Gupta), Freedom Fighter who could be regarded as not only the Founder but also as the "Father of Vasavi Movement"

Schemes Details

KC Gupta Fellow (KCGF) – Corpus Fund

KCGF – is a corpus fund created with specific objective of meeting educational need of poor students by way of financial assistance, distribution of notebooks, helping in any other mode to the poor students of the society in their educational need through the program called VasaviSaraswathiPadhakam. Also during the time of natural calamities, special assistance will be provided from this fund to the deserved. The contribution for this fund will be received from any member of Vasavi Clubs International who is willing to contribute Rs.2500/- a fixed sum. All such contributions received with a specific direction, will be deposited in a specific fixed deposit and the interest from specific deposits will be used for the objectives of KCGF Fund.

Vasavi Kutumba Suraksha Padhakam

VKSP - VasaviKutumbaSurakshaPadhakam is a benevolent fund for the members of Vasavi Clubs International. Any Vasavi Club member from any Vasavi Club in good standing is eligible to join this fund. Any member who intends to join this fund has to remit a non refundable amount of Rs.3000/- to this fund out of which Rs.1000/- is towards Admission Fee which will be deposited permanently under FDs in the bank and the interest earned on such FDs will be utilized for administration purpose only, without disturbing the FDs at any time. The other Rs.2000/- is towards contingency liability of the member. If any member in this fund demises, an intimation by way of SMS will be given to all the remaining members and an amount of Rs.100/- for natural demise or Rs.200/- for accidental demise will be deducted from the contingency liability amount of each member in VKSP Normal and the deducting amount gets doubled in VKSP Senior. This collected amount will be provided as welfare assistance to the family members of the demised member. Any member in VKSP is eligible for benefits only after 2 years from the date of joining in VKSP Normal and only after 1 year from the date of joining in VKSP Senior.

Vasavi Saraswathi Padhakam

The KCGF contribution amounts are being deposited permanently under FDs in Banks from the day one and only the interest earned on FDs will be utilised during the time of Natural Calamities and to serve the student community under the banner VasaviSaraswathiPadhakam. Under VasaviSaraswathiPadhakam, educational assistance is being provided to the deserving students through our member clubs in good standing. In addition, notebooks and dictionaries are also being distributed to poor students. Educational assistance will be provided to most deserving individual students on merits of each case.This assistance will be provided through our member clubs in good standing.

Vasavi Self Employment Scheme

Self Employment Scheme was introduced in the year 2004-05 to Provide Self Employment to Economically backward families with Rs.20,000/- as Interest free loans to start Business/to improve their existing business. The above loans are Provided with the Financial help of GMR Varalakshmi Foundation; Vankadara Vasudeva Rao, Ratnakumari, Dharmika, Nidhi, Crane Foundation, Sidda Surya RrakashRao foundation & Kallepalli Chalapathi Rao, Swayam Samvruddhi Scheme.In addition to this Vasaviclubs are extending Self Employment by providing sewing Machines, wet Grinders & Financial assistance to the needy. From 2020, the loan amount of Rs.20,000/- has been increased to Rs.25,000/-. Clubs can apply for Self Employment Scheme in Form No. 1019 along with photo and ID Proof of applicant. The Club President, Secretary, Treasurer & Project Chairman should stand as guarantors for the loanees. The beneficiaries should submit self cheques along with Application Itself are to be recommended by Governor, taking into consideration of the Service Activities / Performance of the respective Clubs. Cheques will be sent in the name of beneficiaries through clubs.

Sreyobhilashi Scheme

Considering the requests from Vasavians, receiving donations to provide Self Employment Loans to economically backward families Sreyobhilashi Scheme was introduced in the year 2019 to Provide Self Employment to Economically backward families with Rs.20,000/- as Interest free loans to start Business/to improve their existing business. 463 Sreyobhilashi contributions were received in 2019.
